About Sealoace

Sealoace (GenAm: /'siːloʊs/, RP: /'siːləʊs/) is a talentless dweeb who overanalyzes everything to the point of non-existence. Paralyzed by shame of his wasted past and tormented with fear of his hopeless future, this miserable, humorless and not particularly bright individual thinks of himself so highly and thirsts for so much attention that he is willing to buy webspace with money he does not have, only to share poorly conceived and hardly original mental excrescences on film and television.
